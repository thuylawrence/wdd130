Sitting around a bonfire with families is one of the favorite activities. Listening to stories, reminisce our childhood, roasting mashmellows are simple, yet brings everyone joy and peace.

When you feel adventurous, ziplining will give you an experience of a lifetime. Sitting securely up in the air as you sliding through the tree line, you can enjoy the view from uptop and feel the wind in your hairs.

As for the little ones, there will be scavenger hunt with fun surprise and sweet to the taste. Little ones will explore the surrounding, learning about natures, and finding healthy treats along the way.
