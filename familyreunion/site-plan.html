Content

Memories

As time goes by, our lives get busier and busier. We slowly drift apart from each other. We don't get to see each other as often as we'd like. Some of us live overseas, some of us live in different states. Perhaps meeting each other once a year will be a perfect way for us to see each other, to hold each other, and to spend time together. It is a time for cousins to play games, to sing songs, to dance, and to enjoy each other's company. Let's make each of us feel that we belong to a loving and caring family.

Images for the Home page

Fun Activities

Popular outdoor and indoor activities are available for all ages. Itinerary for our one week family reunion trip includes camping, hiking, fishing, and a talent show. These activities will provide us with opportunities to enjoy nature, to exercise, to relax, and to enjoy each other's company. There are a few places to choose from. Please give us some feedback on where you would like to go.

Feedback

We need your feedback on the date, the destination, and the activities you want to participate in. This is a once-a-year event for our family to get together. Our hope is to provide a memorable time for all. If there are any questions regarding the date, the destination, or the activies, please email Thuy at thuyduonglawrence@gmail.com. We hope to see you all there!
